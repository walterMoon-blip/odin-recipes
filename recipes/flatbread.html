Flatbread

Description

Why eat cardboard for dinner, when you could make delicious fladbread pizza at home?

This recipe can be as simple or complicated as you like.

Ingredients

Crust
  • 1 1/2 cups Flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp Yeast
  • 3/4ish cup Water
  • 1 T Sugar
  • 1-2 T Oil
  • 1 tsp salt
Toppings
  • Mushroom
  • Peppers
  • Onion
  • Salami
  • Cheese
  • Marinara

Steps

  1. Mix warm water with sugar and yeast
  2. Let sit for a few minutes until frothy
  3. In a separate bowl, mix flour and salt
  4. Stir wet and dry ingredients together, along with 1 T oil
  5. Make a ball, place in oiled bowl and cover
  6. Let sit for an hour in a warm place
  7. After the rise, stretch and flatten dough to whatever shape
  8. Use the back of a spoon to spread sauce to the edge of dough
  9. Top with shredded cheese
  10. Top with sliced bits of whatever you want. I like onion, peppers, mushroom and salami.
    Hot Clue: place salami on top; it will infuse flavour and crispiness to the vegetables
  11. Place into 425 oven for 10-20 min
  12. If you want extra crisp, place under broiler for a couple min at the end. Lots of toppings take a bit longer to cook
